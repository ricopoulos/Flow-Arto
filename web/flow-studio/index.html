Why Flow Studio?

AI tools generate designs. Flow Studio generates design systems.

Templates, not brands

Most AI design tools generate generic, templated UIs that look obviously AI-made. Every output feels like it came from the same mold, ignoring your brand's unique voice and visual identity.

Generate, then fix

You spend more time correcting AI mistakes—adjusting spacing, fixing contrast, aligning to your brand—than you save. The promise of automation becomes a cycle of manual refinement.

Inconsistent across projects

AI tools forget. Each generation is isolated, with no memory of your design tokens, brand constraints, or past decisions. Maintaining consistency across projects becomes impossible.

The Flow Studio System

Five specialized agents. One coherent system.

Arto

UX Strategist • Layout & Hierarchy

Arto reads your brand config and creates information architecture that aligns with your brand's density, tone, and emotional goals. Structure before style.

Stylist

Design Polish • Tokens & Visual Coherence

Stylist translates your brand definition into concrete design tokens—colors, typography, spacing, shadows. Every value is intentional and documented.

Builder

Implementation • Semantic HTML & Components

Builder creates clean, accessible, token-driven components and pages. Semantic HTML, responsive layouts, no hardcoded values.

Curator

Evaluator • Quality Assurance

Curator evaluates brand alignment, accessibility compliance, visual consistency, and token usage. Catches issues before you see them.

Maestro

Orchestrator • Workflow Coordination

Maestro coordinates the agents, ensures the right brand config is used, and orchestrates iterative refinement based on feedback.

Systematic design, not magic

1

Define Your Brand

Create a machine-readable brand config (JSON) defining your voice, audience, emotions, visual direction, and constraints. This is your source of truth.

2

Agents Generate Systematically

Arto creates structure, Stylist generates tokens, Builder implements components—all reading from your brand config. Every decision is traceable and intentional.

3

Iterate with Curator + Feedback

Curator evaluates the output. Design Interpreter translates your feedback into structured changes. Agents refine. No fuzzy "make it better" loops.
